# StratIQ — Venture Intelligence Platform

> AI-powered platform for business scoring, data analysis, and investor-grade pitch deck generation created by Vidya Balachander and Sumedha Komawar.

---

## Overview

StratIQ is a single-file web app with two modes: Founder and Investor. Founders may use it to evaluate their business against a structured investment rubric, spot strategic gaps through data visualisations, and put together a 15-slide pitch deck they can actually send to investors — exported as PowerPoint, PDF, or plain text. Investors use it to run due-diligence teardowns on incoming decks, flag red flags, and score fit before taking a meeting.

---

## Features

### Scoring Engine
- **100-point rubric** across 6 weighted dimensions
- Instant grade: Investor-Ready → High Risk
- Angel funding probability estimate
- Top strengths, key weaknesses, and priority actions
- Single biggest bottleneck diagnosis

### 6 Scoring Dimensions
| # | Dimension | Max Points |
|---|-----------|-----------|
| D1 | Problem & Market Opportunity | 20 |
| D2 | Product & Solution Strength | 20 |
| D3 | Business Model & Unit Economics | 20 |
| D4 | Go-to-Market Strategy | 15 |
| D5 | Team & Execution Capability | 15 |
| D6 | Innovation & Competitive Edge | 10 |

### Data Analysis
- Dimension score bar chart vs. industry benchmark
- Radar profile chart
- 6 KPI summary cards
- AI-generated strategic insights per dimension

### Pitch Deck Generator
- 15 investor-grade slides pre-filled from your submission
- Inline rubric guidance per slide
- Progress tracker
- Fully editable before export

### Scoring Rubric Detail

Each dimension uses a 4-tier band:

| Tier | Score Range | Meaning |
|------|-------------|---------|
| Excellent | 90–100% of dimension max | Investor-ready on this dimension |
| Strong | 70–89% | Solid with minor gaps |
| Adequate | 50–69% | Present but needs development |
| Weak | < 50% | Critical gap — address before fundraising |
